My Dream Job: Doctor

Why I Chose This Job

My dream job is to become a doctor. I chose this job because I want to save lives and help people feel better. I also love science and learning about the human body.

Advantages

  • Doctors save lives and help people every day.
  • They are respected in society.
  • They always learn new things in medicine.
  • They can make their families proud.

Disadvantages

  • Doctors work for long hours.
  • It can be very stressful.
  • Studying medicine takes many years.
  • Sometimes it is sad when patients do not get better.

Conclusion

Even though it is not easy, I still want to be a doctor because helping people is more important than anything else. It is my dream, and I will work hard to make it come true.
